Ideal Jobs

There are a lot of similarities with each team member's ideal jobs. Firstly every job requires a University degree and IT/computer science qualifications. Every job requires IT and technical knowledge and experience, however each job requires it at a different level. Johnny’s ideal job is more business related, so it requires a less extensive IT skill set in comparison to Mylaad’s and Phannavy’s jobs which requires an exceptional IT skill set with extensive experience. Otherwise all roles require IT knowledge and expertise. Some jobs require more client interaction than others, and drive with the purpose of client deliverables and satisfaction. Moreover, some jobs also require more project and team based work than others that are more individual work based. All of the jobs are looking for hard workers with great people skills, with the ability to communicate efficiently, work in teams, and lead and manage projects, teams, deliverables etc. Three years is the minimum years of experience required across all roles, with the exception of Phannavy’s requiring seven plus.
